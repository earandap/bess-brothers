Frequently Asked Questions

How long does window installation take?

Most window installations are completed in 1-2 days, depending on the number of windows. We work efficiently while ensuring quality installation.

What warranty comes with new windows?

Pella windows come with a lifetime limited warranty on the frame and sash, 20-year warranty on glass, and 10-year warranty on hardware. Our installation is also fully warranted.

Are tax credits available?

Yes! Qualifying ENERGY STAR windows can earn you up to $600 in federal tax credits. We'll provide all necessary documentation for your tax filing.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options including 0% interest for qualified buyers. Ask about our monthly payment plans during your consultation.
