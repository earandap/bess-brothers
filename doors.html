Frequently Asked Questions

How long does door installation take?

Most door installations are completed in 4-6 hours per door. Entry doors may take slightly longer due to additional security features and hardware.

What materials are best for doors?

Fiberglass doors offer the best combination of durability, energy efficiency, and low maintenance. Steel doors provide excellent security, while wood offers classic beauty with more maintenance required.

Can you match my home's style?

Absolutely! We offer hundreds of door styles, colors, and hardware options. Our mobile showroom brings samples to your home to ensure perfect coordination.

What security features are available?

Modern doors include multi-point locking systems, reinforced frames, impact-resistant glass options, and smart lock compatibility for maximum security.
